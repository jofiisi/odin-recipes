Spaghetti with tomato sauce

Spaghetti with tomato sauce is another one of the essentials
everyone should know about.

Ingredients

  • A can of tomato sauce
  • 500g of spaghetti
  • 5 cloves of garlic
  • Salt, pepper and oregano
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cook the pasta according to the packaging and strain after.

  2. In a pot add olive oil, 4 garlic cloves and some oregano.
    Cook for 20 seconds then turn off the heat.

  3. Add the drained pasta to pot with the garlic inside,
    the tomato sauce, half a teaspoon of salt, some pepper
    and another clove of garlic. Stir until its warm,
    and now enjoy!
