Delicious Meatballs

Meatballs are one of the essentials everyone should know,
so I am gonna teach you how to make them.

Ingredients

  • 500g of lean meat
  • 125g of breadcrumbs
  • 62.5g of milk
  • 1/2 teaspoon of salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon of Worcestershire sauce
  • 1/4 teaspoon of pepper
  • 1 small onion, finely chopped
  • 1 egg

Steps

  1. Heat the oven to 200C. Put foil into an 33 by 22cm pan and oil it up.

  2. In a large bowl, mix all ingredients. Shape mixture into 24 meatballs
    2 1/2 cm apart.

  3. Bake 18 to 22 minutes or until temperature reaches 70C internally,
    or until its not longer pink inside.
